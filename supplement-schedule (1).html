⚠️ Important Reminders
◆AHCC — Take 30 minutes before breakfast and dinner on an empty stomach for best absorption
◆Zinc & Copper — Never take together. Zinc at breakfast & dinner only. Copper at lunch only
◆5-HTP — Do NOT take if you are on any antidepressant or SSRI medication
◆Fat-soluble supplements — Omega-3, D3+K2, Beta-Carotene, and Curcumin must be taken with a meal containing healthy fats
◆Blood work — Get baseline zinc + copper + CBC levels before starting, recheck at 6 weeks
